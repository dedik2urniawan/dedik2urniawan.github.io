Tentang

Saya adalah nutrisionis dan analis data yang bekerja di Dinas Kesehatan Kabupaten Malang, dengan rekam jejak panjang dalam pengembangan program gizi masyarakat, surveilans status gizi, dan inovasi pelayanan publik berbasis data dan teknologi digital. Latar belakang pendidikan gizi klinis dan masyarakat dipadukan dengan keahlian pemrograman, statistik, dan business intelligence untuk mendesain solusi kesehatan yang terukur, replicable, dan evidence-based.


Ketertarikan utama saya adalah menghubungkan data, teknologi, dan kebijakan: mulai dari pemantauan pertumbuhan balita, pemetaan partisipatif komunitas, hingga pengembangan dashboard analitik untuk mendukung keputusan strategis penurunan stunting di level kabupaten maupun nasional.

Profile

I am a nutritionist, data analyst, and public health innovator at the District Health Office of Malang, Indonesia. My work focuses on nutrition surveillance, data-driven decision-making, and digital health innovation to accelerate stunting reduction and strengthen primary health care systems.

Key Expertise

  • Nutrition data analytics (children under five, pregnant women, adolescents).
  • Design and implementation of nutrition surveillance systems (CPMS, SSGI, EPPGBM).
  • Dashboard and BI development (R, Python, Looker Studio, Power BI, Streamlit).
  • Digital health innovations for integrated public health services.

Flagship Innovation

RCS Dashboard & TINDIK ANTING AI is a county-wide nutrition surveillance platform that integrates multiple data sources into a single decision-support dashboard. It combines Community Participatory Mapping & Surveillance (CPMS) with AI-assisted risk scoring to help health workers detect and prioritize high-risk stunting cases.
